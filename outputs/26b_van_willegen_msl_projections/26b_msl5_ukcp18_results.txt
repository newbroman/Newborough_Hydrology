Script 26b — UKCP18 MSL5 climate projections
============================================================

Method: Monthly Δh perturbation overlay on observed climatology.
Δh(m) = β₁·(P_scen(m) − P_base(m)) − β₂·(PET_scen(m) − PET_base(m))

This is NOT a forward time projection. The trajectories
show what observed MSL5 over 2014–2025 would have been
under each UKCP18 scenario's perturbed climatology.
